# Capstone Process Format Guide

## Process No. and Title
- Each process should have a unique identification number followed by a succinct title that summarizes the purpose of the process.

## Flowchart
- **Applicable Formats:** HYBRID and LONG processes should include a flowchart to visually represent the steps involved.
- **Note:** Flowcharts are not required for SHORT processes.

## Overview/Description
- A brief overview or description—between 1 to 2 paragraphs—should follow the title, summarizing the main objectives and steps of the process.
