What is Code Kombat?

Code Kombat is a gamified web-based educational platform designed to teach programming fundamentals through interactive challenges, real-time feedback, and engaging visuals. Whether you're a beginner or a curious learner, embark on coding adventures where logic becomes your superpower.

Interactive Challenges

Write, run, and solve programming puzzles with built-in feedback and guidance.

Game Modes

Practice, compete, or take quizzes—all gamified to boost your coding skills.

Track Your Progress

Advance through levels, earn points, and rise on the leaderboard!
